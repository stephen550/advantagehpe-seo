Signs You Need Help Now

  • AC running but not cooling — The most common complaint we get from Okaloosa County homeowners, especially once temperatures climb into the 90s.
  • Warm air blowing from vents — Could be a refrigerant leak, a failing compressor, or a problem with your ductwork.
  • Unusual noises — Grinding, banging, or squealing sounds mean something mechanical is failing inside your unit.
  • AC cycling on and off frequently — Short cycling puts extra strain on your system and drives up your electric bill.
  • Water leaking around your indoor unit — A clogged condensate drain line is common in Fort Walton Beach's humid climate.
  • Thermostat not responding — The issue may be the thermostat itself, the wiring, or the system it controls.
  • Spike in your FPL bill — A sudden increase in energy costs often points to a system that's working harder than it should.

The most common causes we see in Fort Walton Beach homes are low refrigerant (often from a leak), a dirty or frozen evaporator coil, a failing compressor, or restricted airflow from a clogged filter. We diagnose the specific cause before recommending a repair.
