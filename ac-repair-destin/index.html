Do you provide emergency AC repair in Destin?

How much does AC repair typically cost in Destin?

It depends on the issue. Minor repairs like a capacitor replacement are on the lower end, while compressor or coil replacements cost more. We provide upfront pricing after diagnosing your system — you'll know the exact cost before any work begins. No hidden charges.

When should I replace my AC instead of repairing it?

Consider replacement if your system is over 12-15 years old, requires frequent repairs, uses R-22 refrigerant (which is no longer manufactured), or if the repair cost exceeds roughly half the price of a new system. We'll give you an honest recommendation based on your specific situation. See our AC replacement and installation options.

Why does my AC run all day but my house still feels humid?

This is one of the most common complaints in Destin. Your AC may be oversized (cooling the air too quickly without removing enough moisture), your system may have a refrigerant issue, or your drain line may be partially blocked. A proper diagnosis will pinpoint the cause.

How often should I service my AC in Destin?

We recommend at least one professional tune-up per year — ideally in the spring before Destin's long cooling season starts.
