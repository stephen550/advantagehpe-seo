What Flickering Lights Can Mean

  • Lights flicker when large appliances like AC or dryer turn on
  • Flickering occurs throughout the house, not just one room
  • Lights dim and brighten rhythmically
  • Flickering is accompanied by buzzing from outlets or switches
  • You've noticed warm or discolored switch plates
  • Flickering started after a storm or power surge
  • Your home is 30+ years old with original wiring

Frequently Asked Questions

Are flickering lights dangerous in my Destin home?

They can be. Loose wiring connections — the most common cause of whole-home flickering — create arcing that is a leading cause of residential fires. If multiple lights or rooms are flickering, call an electrician the same day.

What causes lights to flicker when the AC turns on in Destin?

A brief flicker when large appliances start is normal — that's the startup current draw. Prolonged dimming or frequent flickering suggests an undersized circuit, a failing breaker, or a loose connection at the panel.
