Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency AC repair in Niceville?

Yes. We provide emergency service when your AC fails and waiting isn't safe or practical — especially during summer heat. Call us anytime.

How much does AC repair cost?

It depends on the problem. A capacitor replacement costs significantly less than a compressor repair. We diagnose your system first and give you the exact price before starting any work.

Should I repair or replace my AC?

If your system is under 10 years old and the repair is less than half the cost of replacement, repair usually makes sense. For systems over 12-15 years — especially those using R-22 refrigerant — replacement is often the better investment. We'll give you an honest recommendation. Learn more about our replacement options.

Why does my house feel humid even when the AC is running?

Common in Niceville. Your system may be oversized (cooling too fast without removing enough moisture), low on refrigerant, or have a partially blocked drain line. A proper diagnosis will identify the cause.

How often should I have my AC serviced in Niceville?

At least once a year — ideally in spring. Homes near the bayous or under heavy tree cover may benefit from twice-yearly service. Our Andy's Advantage Plan covers annual maintenance.
