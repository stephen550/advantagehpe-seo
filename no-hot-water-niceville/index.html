Signs Your Water Heater Is Failing

  • No hot water at all — even after waiting
  • Water gets warm but never hot
  • Hot water runs out much faster than it used to
  • Rumbling, popping, or banging sounds from the tank
  • Water around the base of the heater
  • Rusty or discolored hot water
  • Your water heater is 10+ years old
Niceville & Okaloosa County: Niceville's hard water accelerates sediment buildup in water heaters. Annual flushing extends tank life significantly — Okaloosa County homeowners with untreated water often see tank failure 3–5 years early.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I suddenly have no hot water in my Niceville home?

The most common causes are a failed heating element (electric), a tripped breaker, a failed thermocouple or pilot light (gas), or a tripped high-limit switch. Our plumbers diagnose the issue within minutes of arrival.

Can my water heater be repaired or does it need replacement?

Most water heaters under 10 years old can be repaired cost-effectively. Units over 10–12 years old with major failures are usually better replaced. We'll give you an honest assessment and let you decide.
