Signs You Need a Mini-Split System

  • A room or addition is always hot no matter what the thermostat says
  • You have a garage, sunroom, or bonus room with no ductwork
  • Your home has no existing duct system
  • You want independent temperature control for specific rooms
  • Your window AC units are loud, inefficient, and block light
  • You're finishing a basement, attic, or detached structure

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does mini-split installation take in Destin?

A single-zone mini-split installation typically takes 4–6 hours. Multi-zone systems or installs requiring longer line set runs may take a full day. We handle permits in Okaloosa County when required.

What size mini-split do I need for my Destin space?

Sizing depends on square footage, sun exposure, insulation, and ceiling height. As a rough guide, most rooms need 9,000–18,000 BTU. We perform a proper Manual J calculation before recommending a unit.

Do mini-splits work well in Florida humidity?

Yes — modern mini-splits include dehumidification modes that work exceptionally well in Florida's climate. Many Destin homeowners report their mini-split handles humidity better than their central system.
