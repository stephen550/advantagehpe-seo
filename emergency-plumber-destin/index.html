My home smells like sewage. Is that an emergency?

It can be. Sewer gas contains hydrogen sulfide and methane, which are both health hazards. If you smell sewage inside your Destin home, call us for same-day service. The cause is usually a dried-out trap, a cracked sewer line, or a backup forming in your main line.

How do I shut off the water to my house?

Your main water shutoff valve is typically located near where the water line enters your home — often in the garage, near the water heater, or on an exterior wall. Turn it clockwise to close. If you can't find it, we can walk you through it over the phone.

Does homeowner's insurance cover plumbing emergencies?

Most policies cover sudden water damage (like a burst pipe) but not gradual leaks or maintenance issues. Document the damage with photos before cleanup and contact your insurer. We provide repair documentation that supports your claim.
