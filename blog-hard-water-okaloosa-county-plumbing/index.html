Hard Water in Okaloosa County: What It Does to Your Plumbing

If you live in Fort Walton Beach, Destin, Niceville, or anywhere in Okaloosa County, your home has hard water. The municipal supply comes from the Floridan Aquifer — deep groundwater that passes through limestone bedrock before reaching your tap. That limestone dissolves calcium and magnesium into the water, and those minerals cause real damage to your plumbing system over time.
