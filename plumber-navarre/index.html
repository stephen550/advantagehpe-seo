How much does a plumber cost in Navarre?

Costs vary by the job. A drain clearing costs less than a sewer line repair. We provide upfront pricing after diagnosing the problem — you'll know the cost before any work begins.

How do I know if I need to repipe my home?

Signs include discolored water (rust-colored), low water pressure throughout the house, frequent pinhole leaks, and visible corrosion on exposed pipes. If your Navarre home was built before 1990 and has galvanized or polybutylene pipes, a plumbing inspection is a smart investment.

Is Navarre water hard?

Yes. Santa Rosa County water has elevated mineral content. Hard water causes scale buildup in pipes, water heaters, and fixtures. A water softener or filtration system can help — ask us about options.

What should I do if I smell gas in my home?

Leave the house immediately. Do not flip any switches or use your phone inside the home. Once you're safely outside, call us and your gas utility. We're licensed for gas line detection and repair.
